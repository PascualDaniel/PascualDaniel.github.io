Aftermath Fellows

Aftermath Fellows

Lead a growing squad of brave little helpers as they rescue survivors, clear debris, repair damage, and push deeper into disaster zones. Manage your crowd of Fellows, keep them safe from hazards, and complete objectives to restore hope one area at a time.

Theme: Weathering the Storms

Play it on itch.io: Aftermath Fellows

My Contributions

  • Enemy / Hazard AI & Behavior: design and implementation of hazard logic, pathfinding, safe-zone avoidance, danger detection, dynamic reactions.
  • Art Tools: two custom tools for spawning decals, one for simple decals following a spline, another to create a spline model for tracks.
  • Game Mechanics: Enemy and hazard interactions, attacks, vfx calls.
  • VFX & Visual Feedback: particle effects, animations for debris/repair, feedback for damage & healing, environmental effects (rain,dust), UI/HUD effects.

Key Features & Mechanics

  • Squad-based control manage multiple Fellows at once, assign tasks, prioritize rescue or repair.
  • Dynamic hazard system environment dangers (weather, collapsing debris) that require strategic responses.
  • Rescue & recovery loops clear debris, heal survivors, rebuild pathways giving a sense of progress and restoration.
  • Procedural or handcrafted level progression increasing difficulty, changing environments from chaotic disaster zones to restored areas.
  • Visual & atmospheric feedback — storms, debris dust, environmental damage effects, post-disaster mood shifting to hope as you rebuild.
