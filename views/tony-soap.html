Tony Soap - A Game Jam Project

I participated in the 2025 Global Game Jam where we created a game called Tony Soap. In this game, you play as a duck skating with a soap stick on a bathtub. The Jam Lasted 48 hours and we had a lot of fun creating this game. You can find the project on my GitHub repository: Tony Soap.

You can also play the game on itch.io: Tony Soap on itch.io.

On this project, I worked on the VFX and Pixel Art. Some examples are:

  • Created particle effects for the soap trails.
  • Two types of bubbles, one for grabing and one to add to the trail
  • Developed background art and some assets.
  • Most of the materials.
  • The logo
  • The banner art
  • The ducks that appear while doing tricks
  • Parts of the UI like the buttons
