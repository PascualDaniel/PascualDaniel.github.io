Chrisopoeia - A Game Jam Project

I participated in the 2022 Epic Mega Game Jam where we created a game called Chrisopoeia. The theme of the jam was "As above so bellow". Our game, revolves around the concept of transmutation and the alchemical process of turning lead into gold. With an alchemical gun you can transmute the elements of the world to solve puzzles and advance through the levels.

You can find the project on my GitHub repository: Advanced Human Herding.

You can also play the game on itch.io: Advanced Human Herding on itch.io.

This project was made with Unreal and using some free assets. On this project, I worked on part of the gameplay and materials, part of the sounds and designing some mechanics.
