Slide Dungeon - A Puzzle Adventure

Slide Dungeon is a top-down puzzle adventure where you play as a charismatic wizard trapped inside a dungeon. Your main ability is casting electric bolts to activate towers, open doors, and defeat enemies while solving room-based challenges.

What makes the game unique is that as you progress, you unlock rooms that you can later slide around like pieces of a giant puzzle, building your own path toward the exit. The atmosphere blends humor, mystery, and a cartoon chibi art style, giving the adventure a lighthearted and charming tone even amidst the darkness of the dungeon.
