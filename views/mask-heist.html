Mask Heist

A Global Game Jam heist game where every stolen mask grants a new power.

Mask Heist - Global Game Jam 2024

Mask Heist is a stealthy, fast-paced heist game built for Global Game Jam. You play as a thief infiltrating a museum where every mask you collect transforms your abilities—turning stealth into speed, shadow tricks, or powerful escapes.

I created all of the pixel art, supported the technical art pipeline, and produced the team throughout the jam. The art direction leans into moody museum lighting and bold silhouettes, while the mask designs showcase each new power.

Play the game on itch.io: Mask Heist on itch.io.
